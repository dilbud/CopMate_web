Current Procedure

Step 1: The Driver breaks a traffic rule.

Step 2: Police confiscated driver's driving license.

Step 3: Police issue a spot fine statement

Step 4: Driver goes to office and receives a fine payment form from the traffic Division

Step 5: Driver goes to post office and pays fine for which he/she receives a receipt.

Step 6: Driver goes to police station, shows receipt and police return driving license

New Procedure

Step 1: The Driver breaks a traffic rule(s)

Step 2: Police scans drivers driving license and temporarily disable it Using the mobile app

Step 3: Police issue the driver’s driving license to the driver.

Step 4: Driver goes to nearest post office and gives his driving license or the spot fine statement to them and pays fine.

Step 5: post office gets the payment from the driver and gives him a receipt using the web-app.(the receipt will be generated By the web-app

brief indrodution

This is an online mobile application for police officers not to keep driving licence of drivers and issue a fine sheet and drivers can pay that from any post office in the country. Because nowadays drivers have to go to the police station that keeps their driving license when they are caught for breaking driving rules. As an example, if someone goes on a trip from matara to kandy he can be caught “by kandy” traffic police . They keep their licence card at kandy police station until the required fine fee will be paid. So high time wastage and difficulty was faced there when they traveled back to that specific police station for the fine payment. This was discussed on Neth fm what if we have a method to overcome this problem. Now here is the solution that we proposed to overcome that practical problem. Creating an online website and mobile application to put all the drivers details to the system and any traffic policeman can issue a fine sheet for drivers with specifying real mistakes done by the driver using this application that supply the evidence and fall fine sheet issuing will be cancelled through this. And every traffic policeman can view the drivers early status of driving faults and that they paid for the fines by any place in the country. So they don't need to keep their licence because of that and it will be easier for anyone to not waste their time at the police stations. And also we hope to introduce another function to send a direct notification to the nearest police station and hospital easily when a road accident happens. So that will increase the safety of public people through this application. Still there is no solution to solve this problem. so this project will fulfill that practical problem through this solution.
